A tribute site for people who inspire ME

Dr. Disrespect


- Dr Disrespect, who is often also referred to as ‘Dr D’, ‘The Doc’ or ‘The Two-Time Champion’, mainly streams himself in his fictional Champions Club playing FPS games such as Valorant and Call of Duty: Warzone. He is the creator of the ‘Triple Threat Challenge’, a test of gaming skill in which he attempts to win rounds in three different battle royale or FPS titles back-to-back in as little time as possible. He often invites other streamers or professional players to take on the challenge.

Michael Reeves


- Michael Reeves (born November 20, 1997) is a Filipino-American YouTube personality known for his comedy-engineering videos. Reeves previously worked as a software developer in Hawaii. His first video, "The Robot That Shines a Laser in Your Eye" posted in April 2017, went viral.

Pewdiepie


- PewDiePie, also known as Felix Arvid Ulf Kjellberg, is a popular "Let's Play" youtuber who is the most subscribed individual on YouTube with over 109 million subscribers. Although other youtubers before him took the gaming platform beyond the internet, he arguably made YouTube the mainstream video site it's known today.
